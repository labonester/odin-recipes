Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast (1 packet)
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Toppings

  • Pepperoni
  • Cooked sausage
  • Peppers
  • Olives
  • Sauteed mushrooms
  • Canadian bacon
  • Pineapple
  • Onions
  • Fresh Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Make your dough using instant yeast and let it rise
  2. Roll out your dough into whatever size and shape you would like
  3. Add sauce to the pizza dough
  4. Add cheese to the dough
  5. Add toppings
  6. Bake in the oven at 475-500 degrees for 8-10 minutes
